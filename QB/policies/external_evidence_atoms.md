# External Evidence Atoms (WoW)

Rule: Use `External Evidence Atoms` for grounded research from outside sources (podcasts, articles, books, talks, reports).

Hard constraints:
- Store citation + your notes + short excerpt only.
- Never store full transcripts/articles.
- Never output or store secrets.

Where to put atoms in this repo:
- `07_Strategy_Canon_Artifacts/external_evidence_atoms/`

Template:
- `QB/templates/external_evidence_atom.template.md`

Linking rule:
- Every atom must link to at least one assumption or hypothesis (preferred) or to a single concrete artifact with a note explaining why upstream link is pending.
